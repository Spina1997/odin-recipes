Cotoletta alla milanese

Cotoletta alla milanese is my favourite meat dish. The original recipe is with bovin meat but I prefer the version made with chicken or turkey.

You ned few ingredients to make a nice cotoletta:

  • Meat
  • Flour
  • Grounded bread
  • Eggs
  • Salt and peppet
  • Olive oil

The procedure is easy as well:

  • Prepare 3 big dishes: one with the egg, one with flour and one with grounded bread;
  • Take the meat, pass it in the flour, after in the egg and in the end in the brea;
  • Add olive oil in a pan and heat it to 180Â°;
  • Fry the meat until is crispy and golden;
  • Remove the excessive oli with paper;
  • Put salt and pepper.
