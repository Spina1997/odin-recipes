Pasta al pomodoro

If I have to say a word about the dish "pasta al pomodoro" I would say "childhood". Pasta al pomodoro is the most typical italian dish. Is a concentrate of tradition, colors and silmplynes. Italian people usually after summer join with the family to make tomato souce for the next year; is a nice and happy moment to pass with family when all the people woking together to make a nice sauce to use the next yar to enjoy a nice dish of pasta.

To make a nice dish of pasta you really need few ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmigiano reggiano

The procedure of making pasta is really easy as well:

  • Boil a nice quantity of water and put one little hand of salt inside. When the water start boiling put inside 80g of pasta and let it cook for the time indicated in the pasta box;
  • In a pan make "soffritto": put oil, thin cutted onion and pepper;
  • When soffritto is golden put inside a nice quantity of sauce and warm it;
  • Put the cooked pasta inside the pan to mix the ingredients and put a nice quantity of Parmigiano to give it extra flavour;
  • Enjoy it!!!
